About Me

QA Engineer by day, Craftsman by night!

Hello there! Im Moshe Zuckerman, a dedicated Quality Assurance professional during the day, meticulously ensuring digital perfection. At night i move into a world of sawdust and creativity, perfecting my skills as a craftsman.

Precision in Every Pixel:

During the day, I navigate the intricate landscapes of Quality Assurance, Analyzing code and user experiences with a discerning eye. Attention to detail and a commitment to flawless execution define my work as I strive for excellence.

Crafting Dreams into Reality:

In the evening, my passion for carpentry takes over. With a chisel in hand and creativity in my heart, I transform raw materials into unique creations. From woodworking to intricate designs, the nighttime is when I bring imagination to life.

The Perfect Blend:

For me, the worlds of QA and craftsmanship share a common thread — the pursuit of perfection. Both demand dedication, precision, and an unwavering commitment to delivering perfect results. I smoothly blend these skills, bringing a unique perspective to every project. Whether it's ensuring bug-free software or crafting a one-of-a-kind piece, my journey from craftsmanship to QA is a testament to the versatility of skill.

A Passion for Life's Joys:

Beyond my professional pursuits, being a native New Yorker shapes my interests. I enjoy the roar of muscle cars, the soulful notes of jazz and blues, and the warmth of a fine whisky. These passions fuel my creativity and add a unique flair to both my days and nights. I also cherish the joys of family, married to Miri who is an I/O Psychologist, and as a father of Ronnie and Neta, identical twin girls. I enjoy spending time with them watching them grow.In my leisure moments, you'll find me baking delicious treats or relaxing with a cup of coffee in my hand.

Let's Create Magic, Day and Night:

If you're seeking a professional with passion for digital precision, with a desire to learn and evolve and is a team player you've come to the right place. Let's connect and explore the endless possibilities that emerge when the artistic flair of a craftsman meets the structured world of QA.

QA Expert by Day, Craftsman by Night — Let's craft a masterpiece together!
